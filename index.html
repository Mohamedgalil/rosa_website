FAQ

Questions safety engineers ask us

Does RoSA replace our safety engineer or notified body? +

No. RoSA automates the labour: hazard identification, risk assessment, force estimation, documentation. Your qualified person reviews and signs off instead of starting from a blank sheet. Notified bodies stay in the loop; they just get better inputs, faster.

Which robots are supported? +

RoSA is vendor-neutral. The beta covers stationary industrial robots and cobots across brands. Mobile robots and humanoids follow via the Humanoid & Mobile Plugin, in development for 2026.

Which standards does the documentation follow? +

Risk assessment and reduction follow ISO 13849 and ISO 10218. The exported safety dossier is structured for CE conformity, with report formats for OSHA, UKCA, CSA and other regimes.

What does the beta involve? +

You bring one real robot application. We onboard you on the hosted beta, you run the four steps on your cell, and our engineers review the results with you. It's free, and your feedback shapes the product.
